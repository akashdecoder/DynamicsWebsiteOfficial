CODE ARENA 2021

About

CODE ARENA is the first ever National Level Coding Battle to be conducted by Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur. The rules of the battle are similar to that of boxing games i.e. if one among the two opponents finishes the execution of the assigned problem statement before the other then he will be declared as the winner of the round.

Competitive Coding has become a must do activity for an IT Engineer. Keeping this in mind CODE ARENA would give you the opportunity to showcase your skills in hands on battle combats. Combat with the most dreadful coders and beat them.

CODE ARENA is hosted by Team Dynamics Project Oriented Community under the supervision of Department of Computer Science and Engineering. It is associated with the platform partners VsCode, LiveShare, HackerRank and powered by Computer Society of India (CSI).
